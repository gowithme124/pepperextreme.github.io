About Us

Pepper Extreme is a comprehensive service provider specializing in extreme sports. Since its establishment in 2016, we have been committed to providing the highest quality products and services to those who pursue self-breakthrough, challenge limits, and enjoy life.

Company Positioning

Comprehensive Extreme Sports Service Provider

Mission

To provide the highest quality products and services to those who pursue self-breakthrough, challenge limits, and enjoy life.

Vision

To become the preferred brand service provider in the extreme sports field.

Core Services

  • Brand Positioning (Asia-Pacific Region)
  • Product Planning
  • Visual Design
  • Marketing
  • E-commerce Operations
  • Full Media Communication
  • Channel Distribution
  • Warehousing and Logistics

Channel Advantages

Online: Covering multiple social media and sales platforms including Douyin, Taobao, and Xiaohongshu. Focused on self-operated C-stores while collaborating with B-end major clients.

Offline: Maintaining good partnerships with over 160 BMX/MTB dealers and clubs, consolidating existing markets and exploring sales channels.

Development History

2016

With skateboarding, surfing, climbing, baseball/softball, and karate becoming official events at the Tokyo Olympics, Pepper Extreme began to focus on the extreme sports protective equipment sector.

2021

Established a strategic partnership with the emerging all-category protective equipment brand TheHash, becoming its exclusive agent in China.

2022

Participated in product planning, production, brand positioning, marketing, and other aspects, producing brand and product manuals.

2023

Collaborated with TheHash to organize World Skateboarding Day events with over 30 skateboarding clubs nationwide, achieving comprehensive online and offline communication.

2024

Served as the designated supplier brand at the Shanghai Olympic Qualifiers and successfully organized multiple large-scale events and activities.
